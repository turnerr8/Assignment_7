Stairs to Nowhere

Stairs to Nowhere officially titled Stadium Piece is a architecural piece created by Bruce Nauman finished in 1999.

As you walk up the first set of steps headed away from Red Square on the Stairs to Nowhere note how many steps you climb up.

Nauman was inspired by the idea of entry steps which were used for walking, sitting, reading, talking, and served all purposes. He wanted to create something that pulled from the surrounding STEM buildings and marry art and STEM.
